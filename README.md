![](screenshots/snake.png)

- Collect *Nova Sparks* to grow your snake
![](screenshots/food.png)

- Avoid  *Shadow Blocks* and *Laser Walls* to stay alive 
![](screenshots/laser-wall.png)

- Hold *SPACE* to slither your snake faster
